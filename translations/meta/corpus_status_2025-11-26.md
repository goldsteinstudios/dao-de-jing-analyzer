## Key Framework Additions (from cross-thread synthesis)

### 利 (lì) - Recovered Meaning

**Not:** "benefit" or "advantage"
**But:** "path-cutting constraint" - parametric boundaries that shape void into function

Formula: **利 + 無 → 用**

### 可 (kě) - Mathematical Correspondence

**Function:** The imaginary unit i - rotation/possibility operator
**Action:** Enables orthogonal transformation from implicit to explicit

### 若 (ruò) - Appearance Operator

**Function:** Marks frame-dependent perception
**Usage:** X若Y = "X appears as Y" from misaligned observation

### 復 (fù) - Return/Recursion Operator

**Function:** Cycle back to source
**Related:** 反 (reversal), 歸 (return to root)

### 習常 - Practicing the Constant

**Function:** Recursive participation in frame-independent pattern
**Not:** Achieving constancy, but practicing within it

### Perpendicularity Constraint

P→O promotion requires: **∇G ⊥ ∇B**

Recursion only possible at points of perpendicular intersection between gradient and boundary surfaces.

### The Axiomatic Derivation

From "Reality is infinite" → all structural principles derive necessarily:

1. Scale invariance (常)
2. No absolute void (天地不仁)
3. Contrasts co-emerge (相生)
4. Midpoint unoccupiable (玄)
5. Rotation necessary (可 = i)
6. Closure necessary (反 = π)
7. Recursion necessary (自然)
8. Boundaries dimensionless (無)
9. Paths persist (利)
10. Structure accumulates (德)
11. Perpendicularity gates promotion
12. Patterns scale-invariant
