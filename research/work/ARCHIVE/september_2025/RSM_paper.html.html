Abstract

The Recursive Structural Model (RSM) proposes that reality persists through paradox preservation rather than resolution. Beginning with the impossibility of absolute void, the model unfolds: qualities as infinite gradients, unresolvable paradox centers, forbidden linearity, orthogonal recursion, radius stabilization by π, circulation (qì) with a still hub (wú wéi), spherical sufficiency, Euler compression, and recursive proliferation (the “ten thousand things”). The construction is agentless and atemporal. We demonstrate alignment with mathematics (Euler’s identity), geometry (spheres and radii), logic/computation (non-traversability and recursion), physics (dimensional sufficiency and energy ∝ 1/r intuition), and the Dao De Jing (無名之始 / 有名萬物之母).

1. Introduction

Rather than hypothesizing entities or causes, the RSM proceeds from structural impossibilities and their consequences. The initial impossibility is stark: a pure void without its opposite is incoherent. From this, contrast must exist — not as two discrete poles, but as infinite gradients ranging from “infinitely more this-than-that” to “infinitely more that-than-this.” The rest of the model follows by necessity.

2. The Logic Chain (Agentless, Gradient-First)

2.1 Void Forbidden (P0)

The quality of nothingness only exists in contrast with somethingness; a pure void without its opposite is incoherent. Axiom: Void and Not-Void co-emerge as inseparable poles of a gradient.

2.2 Contrast as Infinite Gradients (Y1)

No quality stands alone. Every quality is an infinite gradient from more-this-than-that to more-that-than-this.

2.3 Paradoxical Midpoint (P1)

Each gradient implies a balance locus. Were it to resolve perfectly, opposites would cancel into void. Since void is forbidden, the midpoint both exists and never resolves: P1 is untraversable.

2.4 The Law of Forbidden Linearity

In 1D, crossing a gradient requires traversing P1. Infinite divisibility renders that span unmeasurable; collapse is disallowed. Conclusion: linearity cannot stabilize; recursion is forced.

2.5 Orthogonal Recursion (i) → O1 (無名)

The only admissible move is an independent axis: orthogonality. The operator i enforces the turn, recasting the blocked midpoint into the first usable origin frame, O1 — the nameless hub (無名).

2.6 Heaven–Earth Axes & the Reciprocity Field (G1)

O1 holds the proportional field G1 dimensional, via two orthogonal necessities:

  • yAxis1 (Heaven/天): the contrast gradient axis.
  • xAxis1 (Earth/地): the orthogonal asymptotic axis that keeps the field open.

Structurally, G1 corresponds to reciprocity (e.g., \(X\cdot Y = 1\) or \(X = 1/Y\)).

2.7 π Stabilizes Radii; Diameters Forbidden

The only admissible span is surface→hub: a radius. A diameter would traverse paradox and enable cancellation (forbidden). π binds radius and circumference, making the span knowable via curvature, unknowable as a direct line:

$$ r = \frac{C}{2\pi} $$

2.8 Still Hub (wú wéi), Moving Rim (qì)

At the hub, wú wéi (∂P/∂t = 0) preserves paradox without forcing. At the rim, qì circulates — \(e^{i\theta}\) — sustaining the gradient in motion.

2.9 Spherical Sufficiency (3D)

Local rotation yields a circle; collecting all orthogonal rotations yields a sphere. Non-orthogonal planes intersect/overlap the existing surface; orthogonality prevents collapse. The sphere is the minimal global shell that holds all gradient orientations from a still hub.

2.10 From 無名 (O1) to 有名 (On, n>1)

Choosing a stable orientation on the shell constitutes a named quality (有名). That fixed axis forces the next orthogonal recursion, producing O2 and, in general, On for n>1 — each a named, generative origin.

2.11 Euler Compression

The full engine compresses to Euler’s identity:

$$ e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0 $$

0 forbidden void; 1 minimal contrast; i orthogonality; π circulation ratio (radius↔circumference, no realizable diameter); e continuous recursion.

2.12 Recursive Proliferation (萬物)

Each surface–hub radius defines a gradient orientation relative to paradox; each orientation can recurse, generating the “ten thousand things.” Forms (Rn) are sustained circulations around preserved paradox.

4. Discussion

Necessity, not arbitrariness

Each step is forced by what cannot be (void, 1D spans, diameters) and what must therefore be (orthogonality, circulation, shells).

Paradox preservation

Structure persists not by resolving paradox but by recursing around it — still hub, moving rim.

Dimensional sufficiency

Three dimensions are minimally sufficient for global paradox preservation via radii; more adds redundancy.

Euler as fingerprint

\(e^{i\pi}+1=0\) compresses the operators: 0, 1, i, π, e.

5. Conclusion

無名天地之始，有名萬物之母。

The nameless is the beginning of Heaven and Earth; the named is the mother of the ten thousand things.

The RSM formalizes this line structurally. O1 (無名) opens the dimensional axes that hold the reciprocity field; On, n>1 (有名), are the named origins that proliferate forms. Reality persists because paradox never resolves — it recurses.
