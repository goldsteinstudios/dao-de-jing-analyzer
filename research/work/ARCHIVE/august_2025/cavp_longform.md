## The Operational Framework

CAVP provides clear operational tests for distinguishing structural accuracy from measurement precision:

**A claim of structural accuracy fails if rescaling changes the ratio.** If a supposed invariant changes value under different scales, units, or contexts, it's not structurally accurate—it's context-bound or measurement-dependent.

This test has eliminated many supposed "laws" that turned out to be approximations valid only within limited ranges or contexts.
